ABOUT MARCBEAS

Located in a serene atmosphere and just about 5 minutes drive from the new police barrier off the Mallam-Kasoa road, a 7 kilometers to Kokrobite beach and 10 kilometers to Bojo beach Accra-Ghana, Marcbeas Hotel is reputed as one of conferencing address to Ghana.

Established in 2006, the hotel has literally tried to become one of the finest tourist attractions in the Kasoa. Recent expansion in infrastructure, amenities and services has ensured Marcbeas Hotel has secured a place at the pinnacle its class.

Marcbeas Hotel now boasts 21 rooms plus conference and dining facilities. Refined and celebrated, Marcbeas Hotel is often preferred for its peaceful environment.

Together with its elegant restaurants, bars and a host of other facilities with a team of dedicated professionals committed to making your stay a truly memorable one, Marcbeas Hotel has proudly reinvented itself to enhance its enviable reputation as Kasoa and the entire central region’s Business and Conferencing destination.
