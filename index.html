Located just about 5 minutes drive (2 km) from the new police barrier on the Malam - Kasoa section of the Accra - Cape Coast road, Marcbeas Hotel is known as one of the preferred addresses on the outskirts of Accra.

Our facilities are best in class and we provide excellent hospitality services with a touch of simplicity.

    We offer ...
  • Quiet neighborhood, serene and natural environment
  • Fine local and continental food
  • Rooms with TV, Ceiling Fan, Fridge and Air Conditioning, and with comfortable bathrooms
  • Conference hall with veranda
  • Internet Café


ABOUT MARCBEAS

Established in 2006, with recent expansion in infrastructure, amenities and services, the place has become one of the finest tourist attractions in the area.

Marcbeas Hotel now offers 20 rooms, as well as conference and dining facilities. It is often preferred for its peaceful environment. With a team of dedicated professionals committed to making your stay a truly memorable one, Marcbeas Hotel has proudly reinvented itself to gain its reputation as the central region’s preferred Business and Conferencing destination.

Last but not least some of the best and nicest beaches of Accra are easily accessible, like Kokrobite beach (7 km) or Bojo beach (10 km).
